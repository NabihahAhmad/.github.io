Founder of Sustainable Sister

Nabihah created Sustainable Sister with her sister Nanzeeba back in March of 2022, now having 13 employees. Sustainable Sister is a fashion-tech platform dedicated to bringing accessibility and diversity in fashion. With their upcoming AI-powered software, they plan to change the way people shop.

Personal Stylist/Shopper

Nabihah is a freelance stylist and personal shopper within the NYC area. She curates and manages clients' purchases based on their budget and needs, and offers styling advice to clients to grow and maximize their wardrobe using a virtual closet database.
